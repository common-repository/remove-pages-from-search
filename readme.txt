Tired of search for an one-click solutions for your search results? This plugin will exclude everything except posts from your website's search results.

== Description ==

<p>Simple, yet efficient plugin, in order to keep only posts in your search results.<br>Pages, custom post types and every other type, will be excluded from your WordPress search queries.</p>
<p>Just upload the plugin and activate it. It will take take care of the rest.</p>
<p>* Keep in mind that this plugin works with the default WordPress search engine.</p>

== Installation ==

<p>All you have to do is upload the plugin within your Wordpress Plugins menu and activate it.</p>
<p>Checking your search results after this move and seeing only posts, will verify that plugin is in perfect working state.</p>
<p>In case that you have any support inquiries, feel free to contact me through Support tab.</p>
